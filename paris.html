Museums are generally open from 10am to 6pm, but most museums offer later closing hours until 9pm or 10pm. The museum closures are on Mondays or Tuesdays. However, some monuments are open 365 days a year, and the main ones can be visited until midnight.

Tickets can be purchased at the site or through various travel agencies. It is advisable to buy them in advance during peak tourist seasons.

For first-time visitors to Paris, it's recommended to plan ahead, book tickets in advance for popular attractions, and be prepared for crowds.
